Our main features

Create and Manage Groups

The Create New Plan feature in SavePals allows users to set up customized savings plans tailored to their needs. Users can define contribution amounts, set schedules, and invite friends to join, making collaborative saving easy and structured.

Group Progress Visualization

Users can easily track their contributions, view group performance, and manage their profiles, all in a visually appealing layout designed to keep everyone motivated and on track.

Contribution Management

The Contribution Management feature in SavePals allows users to easily track and manage their savings contributions. It provides a clear overview of each member’s payment status, upcoming dues, and historical contributions.

About SavePals

Savepals was inspired from a traditional savings group. They call it Susu. A group of friends come together to save and each member collects the collective savings of every member in the group after a specified period of time. The round collection and distribution is done for all members. It helps them to improve their businesses or to achive a particular goal. We then stepped to make it digital. This would securing their funds incase the member holding the funds goes missing.
